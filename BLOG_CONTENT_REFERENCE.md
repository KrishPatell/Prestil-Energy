## Section 2: Writing Style Guide

### Tone & Voice

**Primary Tone:** Professional, authoritative, technical  
**Secondary Tone:** Accessible, solution-oriented, data-driven

**Target Audience:**
- Procurement managers and technical buyers
- Field engineers and operations managers
- C-suite executives and decision makers
- Technical consultants

**Voice Characteristics:**
- **Professional:** Use industry-standard terminology
- **Authoritative:** Back claims with data and specifications
- **Technical:** Include detailed technical information
- **Accessible:** Explain complex concepts clearly
- **Solution-Oriented:** Focus on solving problems
- **Global Perspective:** Use metric units, international standards

### Writing Rules

#### Language & Grammar
- **Voice:** Active voice preferred ("The pump delivers..." not "The pump is delivered...")
- **Tense:** Present tense for descriptions, past tense for case studies
- **Person:** Third person ("Prestil Energy's pumps..." not "Our pumps...")
- **Technical Terms:** Define on first use with acronym in parentheses: "Mean Time To Failure (MTTF)"
- **Acronyms:** Spell out fully on first use: "Electric Submersible Pump (ESP)"
- **Numbers:** Use numerals for measurements (25 m³/day), spell out for general numbers under 10 in narrative text
- **Units:** Always use metric units (m³/day, kW, mm, °C, bar)

#### Structure & Formatting
- **Sentence Length:** 15-25 words average
- **Paragraph Length:** 3-5 sentences (50-100 words)
- **Subheadings:** Every 300-400 words
- **Lists:** Use bullet points for features, numbered lists for procedures
- **Bold:** Use sparingly for key terms and important points
- **Italics:** For technical terms on first use, foreign words

#### Content Requirements
- **Data Points:** Include specific numbers, metrics, and specifications
- **Statistics:** Cite performance data (MTTF, efficiency, capacity)
- **Standards:** Reference API, ISO, GOST standards where relevant
- **Examples:** Include real-world applications and case studies
- **Visuals:** Reference images, diagrams, charts where applicable

### Brand Voice Examples

**✅ Good Examples:**
- "Prestil Energy's Ø92mm centrifugal pumps deliver reliable performance with MTTF exceeding 17,500 hours, ensuring extended production uptime in challenging well conditions."
- "Our ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturing facility ensures consistent quality across all production stages, with full traceability from raw materials to finished products."
- "The 24-slot stator design in Prestil Energy's submersible motors provides enhanced efficiency and thermal performance, resulting in improved reliability and extended service life."
- "With 25+ years of manufacturing experience and proven performance across global markets, Prestil Energy combines heritage expertise with modern technology."

**❌ Bad Examples:**
- "We're the best pump manufacturer" (too vague, no proof)
- "Amazing pumps that work great" (unprofessional, no specifics)
- "Our pumps are really good" (subjective, no data)
- "You should buy our products" (too salesy, not educational)

## Section 3: SEO Guidelines

### Keyword Research Process

#### Primary Keyword Selection (1 per blog)
- Choose one main keyword that represents the core topic
- Should have search volume and commercial intent
- Should align with Prestil Energy products/services
- Examples: "submersible centrifugal pumps", "ESP motors", "gas separators"

#### Secondary Keywords (3-5 per blog)
- Related terms and variations
- Long-tail keywords
- Question-based keywords
- Examples: "submersible pump selection", "how to choose ESP motor", "gas separator sizing"

#### LSI Keywords Integration
- Latent Semantic Indexing keywords
- Related concepts and synonyms
- Examples: "downhole equipment", "artificial lift", "production optimization"

#### Competitor Keyword Analysis
- Analyze competitor content
- Identify keyword gaps
- Find opportunities for differentiation

#### Search Intent Mapping
- **Informational:** "What is...", "How does...", "Guide to..."
- **Commercial:** "Best...", "Top...", "Compare..."
- **Transactional:** "Buy...", "Price...", "Quote..."

### On-Page SEO Checklist

#### Title Tag
- **Length:** 50-60 characters
- **Format:** Primary Keyword | Secondary Context | Brand
- **Example:** "Submersible Centrifugal Pumps Guide | Oil Production | Prestil Energy"
- **Placement:** Primary keyword at start

#### Meta Description
- **Length:** 150-160 characters
- **Format:** Value proposition + CTA
- **Example:** "Complete guide to submersible centrifugal pumps for oil production. Learn selection, sizing, and best practices. Request technical consultation."
- **Include:** Primary keyword, benefit, call-to-action

#### URL Slug
- **Format:** lowercase, hyphens, keyword-rich
- **Example:** `/blog/submersible-centrifugal-pumps-guide`
- **Avoid:** Special characters, numbers, dates

#### Heading Structure
- **H1:** One per page, include primary keyword
- **H2:** Major sections, include secondary keywords
- **H3:** Subsections, include LSI keywords
- **Hierarchy:** H1 → H2 → H3 (no skipping levels)

#### Image Optimization
- **Alt Text:** Descriptive, include keywords
- **Format:** "[Product/Topic] - [Descriptive text] - Prestil Energy"
- **Example:** "Submersible Centrifugal Pump - Ø92mm multistage pump for oil production - Prestil Energy"
- **File Names:** Descriptive, lowercase, hyphens

#### Internal Linking
- **Quantity:** 5-10 per post
- **Anchor Text:** Descriptive, keyword-rich
- **Targets:** Product pages, other blogs, company pages
- **Examples:** 
  - Link to products: "View our Ø92mm centrifugal pump range"
  - Link to blogs: "Learn more about ESP system design"
  - Link to company: "About Prestil Energy's manufacturing"

#### External Linking
- **Quantity:** 3-5 per post
- **Targets:** Authoritative sources (API, ISO, industry publications)
- **Examples:** API standards, ISO documentation, technical papers

#### Keyword Density
- **Primary Keyword:** 1-2% of total word count
- **Secondary Keywords:** Naturally integrated
- **Avoid:** Keyword stuffing

#### First Paragraph Optimization
- **Include:** Primary keyword in first 100 words
- **Format:** Natural, contextual usage
- **Purpose:** Establish topic relevance

#### Schema Markup
- **Article Schema:** For blog posts
- **Organization Schema:** For company information
- **Product Schema:** For product mentions
- **Breadcrumb Schema:** For navigation
